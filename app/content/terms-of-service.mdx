## About Momentum

Momentum is a Flutter mobile app for iOS and Android that helps you reflect on your progress one day at a time. Each day, you answer one question — **Did today move you forward?** — and choose a status: Gained momentum, Held steady, or Paused.

The App includes boards for separate tracking spaces, a month-grid view, optional daily reminders, and board statistics. There are no streaks, badges, or gamification.
